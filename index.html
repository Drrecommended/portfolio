I am a Web Developer from Las Vegas, Nevada with working proficiency in HTML, CSS, Sass, Javascript, Bootstrap, Jquery, Node.Js, and Git version control. My ideal work environment is a position where I am making a positive impact on the planet or working in a music-related industry. I am a performing DJ and record collector.
