Technologies:

I am a Web Developer from Las Vegas, Nevada with working proficiency in HTML, CSS, Sass, Javascript, Bootstrap, Jquery, Node.Js, and Git version control. I am also familiar with SQL and NoSQL databases. I graduated from PunchCode, a front-end-development-focused boot camp in 2020 and I am pursuing a career in technology. I am freelancing but also looking for a more professional setting where I can learn the ins and outs of project planning and development on a broader scale and with a team. I believe with my current skill set I would be a great asset to any company.

I am currently bettering my knowledge with ReactJS and design with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. My ideal work environment is a position where I am making a positive impact on the planet or working in a music-related industry. I am a performing DJ and record collector. I love fitness, I enjoy cycling, calisthenics, and weight training. In my free time, I enjoy feeding my brain with books and spending time with my wonderful girlfriend and cat.
